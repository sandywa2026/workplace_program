Tax Tips & Resources

Short tips to help you prepare for lodgement. General information only.

Tip 1: Keep a simple deductions folder

Save receipts or invoices in one place (digital is fine). Small habit, big time saver.

Tip 2: Separate personal vs business spending

Even a basic separation makes bookkeeping cleaner and reduces stress at tax time.

Tip 3: Don’t guess—ask early

If you’re unsure what counts, ask before lodging to avoid messy corrections later.
